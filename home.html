About Us

Patuakhali Polytechnic Institute is one of the best polytechnics in Bangladesh. 1st year class of Diploma-in-Engineering in Electrical Technology in 1991 only 40 It was established with public students and the Institute has since expanded greatly with the growing demand for middle level technical manpower at home and abroad. The institute now offers courses in 5 (five) technologies. Our vision is to be a highly effective polytechnic institute in the country and an innovative provider of higher technical education and our industrial, higher education institution.And to create leading centers of excellence to create sustainable futures for our students as measured and accepted by foreign recruiting agencies.In a changing world. The institute not only produces high level of achievement of professionals but also creates a cohort to serve the next generation.
