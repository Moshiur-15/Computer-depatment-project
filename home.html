ABOUT US

Patuakhali Polytechnic Institute is recognized as one of the leading polytechnic institutes in Bangladesh. It began its journey in 1991 with the introduction of the Diploma-in-Engineering in Electrical Technology, enrolling just 40 students in its first batch. Over the years, the institute has grown significantly to meet the increasing demand for mid-level technical professionals both nationally and internationally. Today, the institute offers diploma programs in five major technologies, providing students with a strong foundation in practical and theoretical skills. Our vision is to become one of the most effective and innovative polytechnic institutes in the country. We aim to be a center of excellence in technical education, closely aligned with the needs of industry, higher education, and international employers. In a rapidly changing world, our mission goes beyond academic excellence — we strive to shape future-ready professionals who are capable of contributing meaningfully to society. The institute not only produces skilled graduates but also nurtures leadership, innovation, and service to the next generation.
